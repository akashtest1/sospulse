What is SendIt?

Send-It is an online tool made for your safety. It can be used to send an SOS message to your emergency contacts. The tool sends an email and a text message to the emergency contacts just with a single click!

Features

  • Fast and meant for your safety.

  • Sends an SOS email to the selected emergency contacts.

  • Sends the latitude and longitude of the user.
